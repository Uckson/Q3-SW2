CHARACTER DESCRIPTION

Edgar

 He wears a long, stone-grey coat with a thick iron grey collar andiron grey trousers which match his shoes.

Lenora

 She wears a long, iron-grey gown with short, puffy grey sleeves with white frills.

Albert

 He wears a white, bishop-sleeved shirt underneath a grey vest with knee-length, steel grey pants.

Juliet

 She wears a long, fern green gown with a square neckline and short, puffy olive green sleeves.

Jon

 He wears a white, long-sleeved shirt with sleeves rolled above his wrists underneath a tortilla brown, standard vest with a large, white ascot tie and tight saddle brown pants.

Professor

 Professor wears a white shirt with a mauve purple bow and a saddle brown coat with a puffy, peanut coat collar which matches with his trousers. The Professor also uses a mustard brown wheelchair.
